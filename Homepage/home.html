About Us

Student's Chapter of IEI CSE - AOT is a university-based community group for students interested in exploring different possibilities in their college life. By joining us students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.


Inter-Hacktive is the first-ever flagship event, an exciting hybrid-mode hackathon, hosted by the community.

Timeline












Registration has already begun! Gather your teammates and get yourself registered. This event is perfect for developers of all levels, providing an ideal platform to showcase your skills, learn something new, and network with fellow enthusiasts.

Our hackathon is designed to challenge participants to develop innovative solutions to a problem or challenge within a set time limit. We kindly request that all participants review our Code of Conduct. Should you have any registration-related queries, please join our discord server and obtain a ticket to speak with the organizers directly.

Join us for the kick-off of the Inter-Hacktive - an epic weekend of coding, creativity, and collaboration! We're ready to unleash the power of innovation and creativity. Round 1 of the hackathon is completely online. All participating teams will have time to make and submit their projects from 10 P.M. on 19 August 2023 to 10 A.M. on 21 August 2023. This is a 36 hour event.

The top teams from Round 1 will be invited to present their projects offline on 24th August 2023 from 11 am to 2 pm.

Exciting prizes including trophies, certificates, goodies and additional prizes offered by our sponsors for the top 3 teams. Winners of each track also be rewarded.
Participation trophies will be given to all.
